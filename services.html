Revisions: Website projects include 2 rounds of revisions during the build process. After launch, ongoing changes are handled through a monthly care plan or quoted separately.

Minor updates: Minor updates include small text changes, image swaps, link updates, button changes, basic content edits, and small layout adjustments.

Larger updates: Larger updates include new pages, redesigning sections, major layout changes, feature changes, or larger structural edits, and are quoted separately.

Monthly plans: Monthly care plans are month-to-month. Plans renew automatically each month unless canceled. Clients may cancel anytime before the next billing date. Updates reset monthly and do not roll over.

Plan changes: Plan changes take effect on the next billing cycle. Current billing periods are non-refundable.

Payment: Website projects require a 50% non-refundable deposit before work begins. The remaining balance is due before the website is launched. Clients may also choose to pay in full upfront.

Service terms: JEM Web Solutions LLC reserves the right to decline, pause, or discontinue services when a project is not a good fit, falls outside scope, or violates agreed terms.
